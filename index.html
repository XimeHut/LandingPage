What's included in your SEO &
Content Marketing audit?

We will tailor the audit to your business, your team's level of SEO knowledge and the competitors that you have, but generally an SEO & Content Marketing audit will include:

  • 01.An expert Search & Content Marketing Consultant with relevant industry experience.
  • 02.A full technical onsite-audit of both your and your competitors website to uncover differences with recommendations to improve the website provided.
  • 03.A comprehensive analysis of your competitors backlinks to identify link building strategies and to build a list of targeted, high quality links.
  • 04.A links analysis of the top 3 competitors ranking for your chosen keywords to find backlinks they share that are missing from your profile.
  • 05.An onsite and offsite content audit to see what content your competitor is creating, how they're utilising it and how it's influencing the SEO strategy as a whole.
  • 06.An industry and competitor content review to see what the top shared and top linked content is in your industry to help ideate on future content that the target market will react to.
  • 07.A 2 hour in-depth audit walkthrough to get your team and any agencies across findings of the audit and to agree on the next step rollout plan your team should follow. Plus a 1-hour follow on meeting as needed.

You'll come away from this process with a comprehensive road map on how your company can incease search rankings and organic traffic using the exact strategies employed by your competitors who are succeeding.
